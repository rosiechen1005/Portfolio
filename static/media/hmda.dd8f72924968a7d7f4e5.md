# FHLBSF Bank Lending Policy Analysis  
**UCLA Policy Brief (Published 2026)**

Co-authored policy research analyzing loan portfolio allocation patterns among 125+ Federal Home Loan Bank of San Francisco (FHLBSF) member institutions across California, Arizona, and Nevada.



## What I Did

- Analyzed 2023 NCUA + FFIEC Call Report data  
- Examined loan composition across residential, commercial, and vehicle categories  
- Evaluated FHLBSF credit line utilization behavior  
- Produced descriptive statistical analysis informing equity and regulatory recommendations  



## Key Findings

- ~65% of institutions draw on FHLBSF credit lines, but only ~27% use more than 25% of available credit  
- Residential real estate dominates portfolios, particularly in California  
- Arizona and Nevada institutions exhibit higher concentrations in commercial real estate lending  



## Impact

- Informed policy recommendations on credit line utilization and portfolio diversification  
- Contributed to UCLA research on financial equity and regional housing access  
- Published through the UCLA Luskin School of Public Affairs
